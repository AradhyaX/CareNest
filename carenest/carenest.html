Common Questions

When should I visit a doctor?

You should have your first check-up as soon as you confirm your pregnancy, followed by monthly visits or as advised by your doctor.

Is walking safe?

Yes, walking is one of the best exercises during pregnancy. However, avoid overexertion and stay hydrated.

What foods should I avoid during pregnancy?

Avoid raw or undercooked foods, unpasteurized milk products, excess caffeine, and alcohol. Always consult your doctor for a personalized diet plan.
